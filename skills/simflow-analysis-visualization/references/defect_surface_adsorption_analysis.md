# Defect Surface And Adsorption Analysis

Use this reference for defect formation energies, charge corrections, surface
energies, adsorption energies, coverage effects, work functions, and slab figures.

## Required Evidence

- pristine and defective/slab/adsorbed source structures, total energies, chemical potentials or reference states, charge/spin state, and correction choices.
- supercell/slab size, vacuum, dipole correction, k-point sampling, convergence evidence, and finite-size assumptions.
- consistent method/POTCAR/basis/potential metadata without copying licensed or proprietary content.

## Methods

- Defects: formation-energy formula, chemical potentials, charge corrections, potential alignment, finite-size caveats, and transition levels.
- Surfaces: surface-energy formula, slab symmetry, area normalization, relaxation layers, vacuum/dipole treatment, and reconstruction state.
- Adsorption: reference molecules/surfaces, coverage, site labeling, zero-point/entropy corrections when used, and energy normalization.

## Claim Limits

Reference-state and correction choices can dominate conclusions. Record formulas
and assumptions before comparing formation, surface, or adsorption energies.
