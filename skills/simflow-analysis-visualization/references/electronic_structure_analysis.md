# Electronic Structure Analysis

Use this reference for DOS, PDOS, band structures, projected bands, optical
spectra, charge-density views, work functions, and related electronic figures.

## Required Evidence

- source files, software/version, predecessor calculation, k-point path or mesh, smearing/broadening, spin/SOC setting, and Fermi-level reference.
- projection definitions, orbital/atom selections, energy zero, units, and whether outputs are raw, interpolated, or post-processed.
- tool route such as py4vasp, pymatgen, PyProcar, VASPKIT-style tools, sumo, custom Python, or user scripts.

## Methods

- DOS/PDOS: energy grid, broadening, Fermi alignment, spin handling, projection completeness, and integrated charge checks.
- Bands/projected bands: path labels, reciprocal coordinates, discontinuities, projection weights, band indexing, and predecessor charge-density context.
- Optical/charge-derived plots: tensor/component choices, local potential or charge integration definitions, and visualization settings.

## Claim Limits

Do not compare gaps, band offsets, optical peaks, or charge states without
recording functional/method choices, alignment conventions, and convergence or
predecessor evidence.
