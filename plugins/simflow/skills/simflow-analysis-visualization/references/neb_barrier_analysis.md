# NEB And Barrier Analysis

Use this reference for NEB, climbing-image NEB, dimer/pathway outputs,
activation barriers, transition states, and reaction-coordinate figures.

## Required Evidence

- endpoint structures, image structures, path order, energies, forces, spring/climbing settings, and convergence status per image.
- predecessor relaxation evidence, charge/spin/method consistency, and any image interpolation or restart history.
- source files and scripts used to compute relative energies and barrier plots.

## Methods

- Barrier plot: reference energy, image indexing, reaction coordinate definition, interpolation/smoothing, and uncertainty or convergence caveats.
- Transition-state claim: maximum-force evidence, climbing-image or TS validation, endpoint consistency, and imaginary-mode/IRC evidence when required.
- Comparative barriers: consistent cell, charge, spin, functional, and correction choices.

## Claim Limits

Do not claim a converged mechanism or activation barrier from incomplete images,
unconverged forces, inconsistent endpoints, or missing predecessor evidence.
