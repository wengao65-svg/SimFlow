# Phonon And Vibrational Analysis

Use this reference for phonon dispersions, phonon DOS, vibrational spectra,
normal modes, dynamic stability, and vibrational thermodynamic evidence.

## Required Evidence

- force constants or displacement/DFPT source files, supercell/q-mesh, masses, units, symmetry settings, and software/tool route.
- structural relaxation evidence, force convergence, acoustic sum rule choices, non-analytical corrections, and imaginary-mode handling.
- source-to-figure lineage for dispersion, DOS, mode visualization, or spectra.

## Methods

- Dispersion/DOS: q-path labels, interpolation, broadening, normalization, projected components, and unit conversion.
- Stability: imaginary frequency threshold, mode localization, structural sensitivity, and convergence checks.
- Vibrational spectra: selection rules, intensities, broadening, temperature, and comparison reference.

## Claim Limits

Imaginary modes and low-frequency branches are sensitive to convergence,
supercell size, stress, and symmetry handling. Avoid stability claims without
explicit convergence evidence.
